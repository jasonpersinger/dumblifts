A Free Workout Tracker That Gets Out of Your Way

No account. No ads. No subscription. Just track your lifts.

Most Workout Apps Are Too Much

You open a workout tracker and it wants you to create an account, pick a plan from a library of hundreds, watch a tutorial, and maybe pay $10/month. You just want to log five sets of squats.

DUMBLIFTS is the opposite. Open it. Tap your sets. Done.

What It Does

Tap to Track

Five set buttons per exercise. Tap them as you finish each set. They turn orange. When all 15 sets are done, complete your workout. That's the whole interaction.

Adjust Weight

Plus and minus buttons adjust weight in 5 lb jumps, matching how fixed dumbbells actually work. No decimals, no kilos-to-pounds conversion, no calculator.

Works Offline

DUMBLIFTS is a Progressive Web App. Install it to your home screen and it works without an internet connection. Your data is stored locally on your device.

Cloud Sync (Optional)

Sign in with Google to sync your data across devices. Switch phones, use a different browser, your workout history follows you. Totally optional. The app works fine without it.

Customize Your Program

The default program is a push/pull/legs A/B split. But you can swap exercises, add workouts, and build the program that works for you. Choose from 20 dumbbell exercises across legs, push, and pull categories.

Progress Charts

See your weight progression over time for every exercise. No complicated analytics. Just a line going up, which is all that matters.

What It Doesn't Do

DUMBLIFTS doesn't count calories. It doesn't have social features. It doesn't send push notifications begging you to come back. It doesn't have a premium tier that locks basic features behind a paywall.

It tracks your dumbbell workouts. That's it.

Who It's For

  • Beginners who want a program that works without decision fatigue
  • Home gym lifters with a set of dumbbells and a bench
  • YMCA / hotel gym users who only have access to a dumbbell rack
  • Minimalists who are tired of bloated fitness apps
  • Anyone who just wants to get stronger with what they've got
